# Crystal Aura
*Default Keybind: NONE*

*Category: COMBAT*

CrystalAura automatically places Obsidian and an End Crystal near a player when they step within the radius of the player. Once it is placed, Aoba will attempt to attack the End Crystal, causing the enemy (9/10) times, to take a fatal amount of damage.

# Settings
 - Radius (Variable): The radius at which CrystalAura will place and hit End Crystals.
