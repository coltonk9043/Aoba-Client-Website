# Freecam

```
Default Keybind: NONE
Category: Movement
```

Freecam detaches the camera from your player and lets you fly around freely, including through blocks and walls.
Your character stays in place while you explore, and other players still see you standing where you were. T
his is useful for scouting nearby areas, inspecting builds from different angles, or looking through walls without actually moving.
