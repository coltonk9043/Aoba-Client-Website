# NoMissDelay

```
Default Keybind: NONE
Category: Combat
```

NoMissDelay prevents the swing animation (and the resulting attack cooldown) from triggering when your crosshair is over air.
This means missed swings no longer waste your attack cooldown, allowing your next real hit to land at full damage.
