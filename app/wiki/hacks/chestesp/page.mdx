# ChestESP

```
Default Keybind: NONE
Category: Render
```

ChestESP highlights chests, trapped chests, and barrels through walls, making them visible even when hidden behind blocks. T
his is useful for locating hidden storage, finding bases, or spotting containers that might otherwise be out of view.
