# AutoSoup

```
Default Keybind: NONE
Category: Combat
```

AutoSoup automatically drinks mushroom soup from your inventory when your health drops below a set threshold. Designed for KitPVP servers,
it will also move soup from your inventory into your hotbar when needed, keeping you healed during fast-paced fights.

- [ ] Vulcan
- [ ] AdvancedAntiCheat

## Settings
| Name | Type | Description | Default |
|------|------|-------------|---------|
| Min. Health | Slider | The minimum health at which AutoSoup will trigger and attempt to consume soup (1.0 - 20.0, Step: 1.0) | 6.0 |
