# Breadcrumbs

```
Default Keybind: NONE
Category: Render
```

Breadcrumbs leaves a visible trail behind you as you move, making it easy to retrace your steps. This is useful for navigating caves,
tracking exploration routes, or finding your way back to a previous location.
