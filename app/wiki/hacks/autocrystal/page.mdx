# AutoCrystal

```
Default Keybind: NONE
Category: Combat
```

AutoCrystal automatically places and detonates End Crystals near enemy players to deal explosive damage.
It intelligently picks placement positions to maximize damage to targets while protecting you with anti-suicide and self-damage limits.
