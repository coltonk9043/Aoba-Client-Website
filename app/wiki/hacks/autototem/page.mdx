# AutoTotem

```
Default Keybind: NONE
Category: Combat
```

AutoTotem automatically moves a Totem of Undying into your hand when your health is low or when an End Crystal is placed nearby,
keeping you protected from lethal damage. It will not interfere while you have a chest or other container open.

## Detectable By
- [ ] NoCheatPlus
- [ ] Vulcan
- [ ] AdvancedAntiCheat
- [ ] Verus
- [ ] Grim
- [ ] Matrix
- [ ] Negativity
- [ ] Karhu
- [ ] Buzz

## Settings
| Name | Type | Description | Default |
|------|------|-------------|---------|
| Health | Slider | The health at which the Totem of Undying will be placed into your hand (1.0 - 20.0, Step: 1.0) | 6.0 |
| Crystal Radius | Slider | The radius at which a placed End Crystal will trigger AutoTotem, causing it to swap a totem into your hand (1.0 - 10.0, Step: 1.0) | 6.0 |
| Mainhand | Toggle | Places the Totem of Undying in the main hand instead of the off-hand | false |
