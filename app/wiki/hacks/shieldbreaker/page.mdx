# ShieldBreaker

```
Default Keybind: NONE
Category: Combat
```

ShieldBreaker automatically swaps to an axe in your hotbar when attacking a target that is actively blocking with a shield, lands the hit to disable their shield, and then swaps back to your previous slot.
Requires an axe to be present in your hotbar to function.
