# AutoEat

```
Default Keybind: NONE
Category: MISC
```

AutoEat automatically eats the best food in your hotbar when your hunger or health drops below a set threshold. It picks the most nutritious food available and can prioritize golden apples over regular food.

## Settings

| Name | Type | Description | Default |
|------|------|-------------|---------|
| Fill To Full | Boolean | When enabled, the module will continue eating until your hunger bar is completely full rather than stopping once it rises above the hunger threshold | true |
| Swap Back | Boolean | Whether your selected hotbar slot will be switched back to the slot you had selected before eating | true |
| Hunger | Float | The hunger level at which AutoEat will trigger (1 - 20, Step: 1) | 10 |
| Health | Float | The health level at which AutoEat will trigger. If your health drops to or below this value, the module will begin eating (1 - 20, Step: 1) | 10 |
| Prioritize Gapples | Boolean | When enabled, enchanted golden apples and golden apples are prioritized over other food items | true |
