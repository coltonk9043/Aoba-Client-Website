# Jesus

```
Default Keybind: NONE
Category: Movement
```

Jesus allows you to walk on the surface of water and lava instead of sinking in.

## Settings

| Name | Type | Description | Default |
|------|------|-------------|---------|
| Legit | Boolean | Whether or not the player will swim as close to the surface as possible. When enabled, the module simulates holding the jump key while the player is in water or lava, causing them to swim upward naturally | true |
