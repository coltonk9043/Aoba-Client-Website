# Reporting Bugs

Found a bug? We appreciate you taking the time to report it. There are two ways to submit a bug report:

## GitHub

Open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Cocolots/Aoba-Client/issues). Please include steps to reproduce the bug, 
your Minecraft version, and any relevant logs or screenshots.

![GitHub Issues](/screenshot_github_bug_reports.png)

![GitHub New Issue](/screenshot_github_bug_reports2.png)

## Discord

Join our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/CceRVcTxpQ) and submit your bug report in the appropriate channel. This is also a great 
place to discuss issues with other users and get help troubleshooting.

![Discord Bug Reports](/screenshot_discord_bugs.png)
