# Alt Manager

The Alt Manager allows you to save and switch between multiple Minecraft accounts without restarting the game. It supports both **Microsoft accounts** and **cracked (offline) accounts**. 
The Alt Manager can be accessed from the Minecraft multiplayer screen.

```
All saved accounts are encrypted and stored locally on your machine. Account credentials are never sent to any third-party servers. Microsoft account authentication goes 
directly through Microsoft's official OAuth service.
```

![Alt Manager](/screenshot_altmanager.png)

## Adding an Account

Click **Add Alt** to add a new account. Enter the email address (for Microsoft accounts) or a username (for cracked accounts), and toggle the **Cracked Account** 
checkbox accordingly. For Microsoft accounts, a browser window will open to complete the login process through Microsoft's official authentication.

## Logging In

Select an account from the list and click to log in, or double-click an account for quick login. Your game session will switch to the selected account immediately. 
The currently logged-in account is displayed at the top of the screen.

## Direct Login

The **Direct Login** option allows you to log into an account without saving it to your account list. This is useful for one-time use or testing an account before adding it permanently.

## Editing and Deleting

Select an account and click **Edit Alt** to update its email or username. Use **Delete Alt** to remove an account from your saved list.
